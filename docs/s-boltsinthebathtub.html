Bolts in the Bathtub

Bolts in the Bathtub is located in the high desert Antelope Valley, home of the California Poppy Reserve. We celebrated five years open in May of 2008. You’ll find 4500 bolts of primarily 100% cotton fabrics, including 500+ batiks. We also carry a selection of rayon, linens, corduroy, silks & twills etc. for clothing and accessory projects. There are lots of samples to inspire your creativity and lots of kits to get you started sewing right away. Our staff is happy to help you select fabrics for any of your projects. We carry Mettler 50 & 60 wt, DMC, Bottom Line, Sulky, YLI, and Superior Rainbow threads. We have all 351 colors of Isacord with 42 new colors on the way. Visit us to test-drive a Bernina sewing machine, serger or jump into embroidery. Bernina embroidery software will enhance your project no matter which brand machine you own. All machine purchases include Machine Mastery classes and the first years’ service. Monthly Software Sampler training and embroidery project classes keep you learning. Classes, Blocks of the Month and Moda University are available or try our annual Challenge, due September 14 - gift cards for the winners. Hope you visit us!
