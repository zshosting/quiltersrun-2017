Inspirations Quilt Shop

Inspirations Quilt Shop has been serving the northeast area of San Diego County since 2007 with high quality quilt fabrics, notions and classes. Our motto is "A Place to Inspire and Be Inspired!" - As our customers inspire us as much as we inspire them. The store hours are: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday thru Saturday and is located at 27350 Valley Center Road, Suite B in rural Valley Center (above Shain’s Fitness). Parking is in the rear of the building with street level entry across the bridge. Contact us at (760)751-9400 or on the web at Inspired2Quilt.com for class schedules and product updates.
