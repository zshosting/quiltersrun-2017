Tops & Bobbins

At Tops & Bobbins, our goal is to offer you a wide variety of designer quilting fabrics and supplies. Our 100% cotton fabrics are selected from top manufacturers such as Moda, Michael Miller, Benartex and more, to ensure that your quilts and sewing projects will last for years to come! We also carry a great selection of books, patterns, kits and sewing notions, as well as professional and timely computerized quilting services.

Tops & Bobbins is committed to providing each customer with prompt, friendly, personalized service.
