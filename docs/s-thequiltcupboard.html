The Quilt Cupboard

We offer a wide variety of quilting and home decorating fabrics, awesome quilting books, museum reproduction quilt patterns, quilting and sewing related gifts. We have a shop cat named Annie - who is a beautiful calico cat. She is approximately 7 years old and loves everyone who walks in the door. Come visit her and the shop. We want to be your "Home" shop. We offer lots of fun and exciting events throughout the year. We have an Open Sewing day every Mon called S.O.S. - or Saving Our Sanity. Come and sew all day, part of the day or a few hours. The cost is FREE and there are always a lot of fun people to hang out with.
